CARDIO HIIT

HIIT:

Burpee

Jumping jacks

Mountain climbers

Jump squats

Instructions:

HIIT stands for high intensity intervall traning. It is a quick and intense form of cardio training. In this workout you only need a timer and some space so you can do it in the comfort of your own home. The workout is done like this: you do one exercise 20 seconds as intense as possible, then you take 10 second break. After that you do the next exercise 20 seconds and rest 10 and so on. You can modify the difficulty by just simply adding more rounds. Good starting point is to do 8 minutes of this in a row and improve from there!:)
