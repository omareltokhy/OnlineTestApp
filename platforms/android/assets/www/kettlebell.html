KETTLEBELL

KETTLEBELL CIRCUIT

Kettlebell swing x10

Left turkish get up x10

Right turkish get up x10

Figure 8 x10

Around the head x5 per side

Squat to shoulder press x10

Instructions:

This is a circuit workout and the purpose is to do all exercises in a row without any breaks. If you need to rest, rest as little as possible. Completing every exercise is 1 round. Do as many rounds as is propriate to your fitness level and take 1-2 minute rest between rounds. Everytime the workout gets too easy just try to complete one more round! :)
